I realized the true core of programming is problem-solving, so this seemed like the perfect career choice for me. Although I started my learning experience in college, I switched from college to We Can {Code} IT bootcamp, and it was the right decision for me. The change was refreshing, the instructors seem to truly care about our success, and the enviroment proved to be conducive to my learning style. This has prepared me for my career as a developer, and I'm excited to see what the future will bring.
