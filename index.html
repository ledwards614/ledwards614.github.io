Hello,

and thanks for stopping by! Here's a little background about myself: I was born and raised in Columbus, Ohio. I spent some time after high school exploring the country which allowed me to live in California, Arizona, and Hawaii, although I enjoyed my travels, I eventually ended back in Ohio. In my free time I enjoy spending my time outdoors. I love hiking, camping, kayaking, and trail running. I also enjoy traveling and love the ocean. I enjoy doing problem solving puzzles in my freetime, such as crosswords, sudoku, and logic puzzles. While I was going to college I was a little bit directionless in what I wanted to study, so I randomly took an entry-level computer science class, and that's when I fell in love with programming. I realized the true core of programming is problem-solving, so this seemed like the perfect career choice for me. Although I started my learning experience in college, I switched from college to We Can {Code} IT bootcamp, and it was the right decision for me. The change was refreshing, the instructors seem to truly care about our success, and the enviroment proved to be conducive to my learning style. This has prepared me for my career as a developer, and I'm excited to see what the future will bring.
